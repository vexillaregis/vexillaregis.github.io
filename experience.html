ABOUT ME 關於我
EXPERIENCE 經驗

Here's some little fun facts about me! I've been travelling around the world on my own since the year I turned 15. Started from Taiwan, I've been to Thailand, Japan, Germany and the Netherlands. Among these beautiful countries, I particularly like the Netherlands. I like the delicious dishes, the nature landscape, the amiable people, the biker-friendly environment and the majestic architecture. I'm as well an amateur photographer and I like to record the little surprises in my life. The digital camera that I'm currently using is the FUJIFILM XT-30 along with the XF18-55mm lenses. I like to take photos in black and white because I believe that in this way the contrast and the construction of the picture could be best shown.

以下是我的一些小經歷！我自從15歲那年便獨自一人旅行。從台灣開始，我去過了泰國、日本、德國和荷蘭。在這些美麗的國家中，我最喜歡荷蘭。我喜歡他們美味的食物、大自然的景色、友善的人們、自行車友善的環境和壯觀的建築物。我也是位業餘的攝影師，而且我喜歡紀錄下生活中的小驚喜們。我現在在使用的相機是富士XT-30的機身搭配18-55mm的鏡頭。我喜歡黑白攝影，因為我認為用這種方式最能展現照片的對比和結構。
