I am Wu, Yu-Hsiu,
currently a junior student in National Tsing Hua University. Recently reached the age 21, I have experienced quite a lot in his university life. I like coding, riding motorbikes, photography and watching movies. As a student in the department of Foreign Languages and Literature, I am able to speak English fluently and I'm currently learning Spanish and Japanese. I've also taken many linguistics courses, including syntax, child language acquisition, phonetics, phonology, semantics, sociolinguistics, and psycholinguistics. Among these courses, I find sociolinguistics being the most interesting, for it reveals the link between social factors and linguistic choices.
