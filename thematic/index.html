Kole's Gallery

Welcome to Kole’s Gallery. Here, you would get to see Kole’s photography works. Started from the year 2019, Kole is still an amateur photographer, as well an artist and a traveler who lives in Taiwan. As a college student, Kole shows the way he sees the world through his FUJIFILM X-T30 digital camera. Having traveled to many places around the world, Kole’s works show his footprint over the globe and in every photo taken underlies a story waiting to be told, so get ready to take on a journey with Kole. Have fun and enjoy!
